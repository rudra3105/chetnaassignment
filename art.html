Art in Early Childhood Education

Art encourages creativity and self-expression in young children, enabling them to explore their world through color, texture, and form.

Resources

Resource Purpose
Crayons, Paint, and Paper For freehand drawing and painting.
Clay and Playdough For exploring 3D modeling and tactile creativity.
Collage Materials For creating textured and mixed-media art.

Age-Appropriate Activities

Examples include finger painting for toddlers and more structured art projects for older children.
